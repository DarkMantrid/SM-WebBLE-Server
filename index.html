To use the Web Bluetooth feature in Firefox on your Android device, please follow these steps:

  1. Open Firefox on your Android device.
  2. Type "about:config" in the address bar and press Enter.
  3. Accept the risk and continue. If you don't get the prompt, see the additional instructions below.
  4. In the search bar, type "dom.bluetooth.enabled".
  5. Toggle the "dom.bluetooth.enabled" preference to true by tapping on it.

If you don't get the prompt to accept the risk when accessing "about:config", follow these additional steps:

  1. Open the Settings app on your Android device.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Apps > Firefox > Permissions > Modify system settings.
  3. Check the app's permissions and ensure that it has permission to modify system settings.
  4. If the permission is not granted, enable it.
  5. Restart Firefox and try accessing "about:config" again.
